About the Dunks Design Craft

After spending my formative years gaining experience in window and door manufacturing, I jumped at the opportunity in 2020 to start Dunks Design Craft. My specialist team and I built a successful business on providing custom wooden doors, windows and mouldings paying close attention to quality. Custom products are the core of our business and DDC prides itself in providing these at affordable prices..
