About Us

After spending my formative years gaining experience in window and door manufacturing, I jumped at the opportunity in 2020 to start Dunks Design Craft. My specialist team and I built a successful business on providing custom wooden doors, windows and mouldings paying close attention to quality. Custom products are the core of our business and DDC prides itself in providing these at affordable prices.

Setup in 2020, "Dunks Design Craft", Dunks Design Craft is a manufacturer and installer of uPVC doors and windows with single, double and triple glazing systems. Our team consists of seasoned professionals that specialize in the field of uPVC windows and doors with more than 1 years’ experience in the industry. Using the highest quality of international materials, hardware and accessories we assure our customers the finest finished product as well as a 10 yr. guarantee on our products and services. Our uPVC profile is manufactured from REHAU which is a German technology that is perfectly suited and compatible with South Africa’s harsh climate. Our upvc profile is WISPECO manufactured, one of South Africa’s leading industry suppliers of upvc with a Level 4 BBBEE rating, which offers 100% recognition as BEE spend. Across India and particularly in the North India, uPVC windows dominate the market and Dunks Design Craft is the number one systems supplier. Dunks Design Craft creates PVC extrusions for windows and doors that set new standards for premium performance and distinctive design. From aesthetics to function and durability, Dunks Design Craft products benefit from quality materials and the most advanced technology. The extensive range meets the requirements of both refurbishment and new construction applications in residential and commercial buildings. With nearly 1 years of expertise in product research and development, Dunks Design Craft has built its reputation on innovative, well-engineered designs. Whether you are looking for single or double glazed windows or doors, multi-fold stacking doors, double glazed sash windows or any other window to suit your home, we can assist you. Dunks Design Craft uses an approved network of specialist fabricators and installers, guaranteeing you world class service.
